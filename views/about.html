What is GO'Act?

Go'Act is a simple social network aiming toward the idea to share posts whenever you make a good action in different domains

Born in the head of IKHLEF Eddy and YUN Eunsun, students in computer science at Aix-Marseille University, for a project with the theme
"make the world a better place".
We thought that making the world better can also consist by simple actions in our everyday life to make the planet a better place. With simples actions from from helping a student to saving animals, the world became a bit better, so if everyone is taking part to theses action, the impact will be huge!
GO'Act! is a project about sharing our good actions and get inspired by others' ones. Thanks to a game like system with multiples leaderboards and a twitter like social network, we aim to make people wanting to participate more in the community, by commenting and support others while making good actions and get encouragement.

About datas and RGPD

GO'Act! is tracking and ads free

GO'Act! follow the RPGD rules, futhermore we have no use of your datas, therefore user data stored in the website will not be shared or sold to anyone.
You can delete your account whenever you want, all your datas will be deleted.
With Go'Act! you are safe.
